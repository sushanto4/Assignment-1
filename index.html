Our Donation Plan

Food

Safe food and water supplies are a major concern following a natural disaster. A disaster like flood creattes a huge crisis of save food and water. First we are gonig to help people by providing them food and water. Without food one can not survive in this situation. Also there are a lot of old age people and babies.

Medicine

During a flood or after a flood necessary medicine are a basic need. Various water borne disease speards in a wide scale. To prevent any kind of epidemic situation we need a lot of medical support. So we are preparing our medical team and medicine support.

Shelter

Shelter is one of the most basic needs for flood effected people. As you know that flood destroy and wash away peoples shelter. They don't have any shed over thheir head. So shelter is very important. We are going to rebuild their houses as far as we can.
